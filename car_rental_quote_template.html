What happens after you return the car:
R
Return day: Vehicle inspected. Final rental charge processed. Bond hold begins releasing.
1-3
Days 1–3: Final charge appears on your statement. Bond may still show as pending — this is completely normal.
10
Up to 10 days: Bond fully released. Your available balance increases. No action needed from you.
?
Still showing after 10 days? Contact your bank — we have already released the hold on our end.
